# Social-Network (MVP)
- the social network is for connecting peoples together. and it separates the roles between users.
- Two types of users:
    - Admin (to make it easy let's not give special task)
        - Can **delete abusive posts/comments**

    - Users
        - **authenticated** and **not auth(Guest)** users.
        - Auth Users:
            - Can **create groups**
            - an **join/leave groups**
            - Can **edit/delete their own groups**
            - Can **create posts** inside groups
            - Can **like/unlike posts**
            - Can **edit/delete their own posts**
            - can **comment** on posts.
            - can **delete own comment** from posts.

            # not for MVP
            - Can **view their own profile**

        - **Not Auth Users:**
            - Can **see groups**
            - Can **see posts and comments** in groups
            - Cannot interact (**like/comment/join/post**)
